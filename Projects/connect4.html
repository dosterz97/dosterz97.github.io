Connect 4

Java Project

Project Goal

Connect 4 was group project final for the AP Computer Science class I was in. In addition to preparing for the AP test, we had to showcase what we had learned in a creative way and work in a group of others. The goal was to create an interactive connect 4 game, that was easy to play and visually pleasing.

Project Outcome

The project became a success, because of teamwork, delegation, and time management. I worked on the game logic and my partner worked on the Graphics. As this project was relatively simple in its problem. It was a great way to prove what we had learned, and replicate something in Java, that I would easily be able to do in C++.
