FBLA: Mobile Application

IOS/Swift Project

Project Goal

In FBLA, the prompt for the mobile application I was required to make was a Digital Yard Sale, similar to any shopping app, to raise money for the National Leadership Conference in Anaheim. I also at the time had an unpaid-internship, in which I used this as my final project for them so I could concentrate my efforts on one single, larger project.

Project Outcome

The app was a success as I took 1st place home in the State Leadership Conference in Springfield Missouri, and I was able to move to a paid internship with the local start-up I am working with. I learned a lot about Swift, the new language for IOS applications, and about competition in an academic way in a competitive environment.
