FreeRide Columbia

Wordpress Website

Project Goal

FreeRide Columbia was a company that needed a website and branding for a small business. FreeRide Columbia wanted a website that could simply and quickly explain what their business was, schedule appointments, and send messages to the owner with comments and concerns.

Project Outcome

The website turned out alright in the end becuase it was able to functionaly send messages to the owner and inform users. I delegated scheduling to another online company, Setmore Appointments, to save time and money for her because the business was handling all the payments through face to face payments.
